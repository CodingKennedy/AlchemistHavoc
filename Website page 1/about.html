I'm Kennedy


I've always lived in #Brooklyn. I have seen my home neighborhood of #Williamsburg change dramatically over the last 15 years.
I see artists of all kinds. I see characters that came right out of a science fiction novel. These things remind me that I am home.

I always want to help people. Let me help you expand your horizons.
