Kennedy


I have always been an artist of sorts. When I was younger I loved to draw and color. I think it was in middle school when we learned different painting styles. That year I won a state competition with a watercolor painting. Even I didn’t know what to think at that! My focus shifted to fiction writing in high school. I do national novel writing month every November. I currently have 3 unedited novels. I know that I am now on the path of my newest creative endeavor. Last January I made the decision to learn how to code. I am extremely happy with that decision. I can use my designer and developer skills, and help you create a place on the Internet for you to express yourself.



I am a psychology major. Understanding other people is something that I enjoy. I see people and try to figure them out. Some are extroverted and out going, with lives filled with color in many different shades. Then there are the introverts, the ones who seem quiet, but their minds are dynamic and filled with deep thought. The websites I build are based on the same themes. Your online presence needs both of those abilities. They need to stand out as if they are a living and breathing person. It needs to represent you or your business in a way that appeals to others. Good business is transparent and shows everyone exactly what they are about.



I am an artist, blogger, writer, dreamer, freelancer, student, human, alchemist, and coder. How do you want to express yourself on the web? Let me help you find out. Send me a message on your ideas! I can’t wait to hear from you!
