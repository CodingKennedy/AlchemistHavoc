    Recently, I have been reading the psychology book “59 seconds : change your life in under a minute” - by Richard Wiseman. There is a writing exercise that I found interesting. It is a way of journaling in a positive and hopeful light. Many of normally get stressed and worry about some aspect in our lives and if we do write these thoughts down, we focus on the bad more than the good. Writing about what ruined your day might even make it worse.
